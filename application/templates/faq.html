Q: What is the difference between an active shooter incidents and a mass shooting?

A: Active shooter incidents are unique because an aspect of the crime may affect the protocol in responding to the scene. Both law enforcement and citizens have the potential to impact the outcome of an active shooter incident. Therefore, all mass shootings are active shooter incidents; however, not all active shooter incidents are mass shootings because of the minimum four deaths to meet the mass shooting criteria.
